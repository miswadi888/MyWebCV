About Me

I'm a strategic, people-centric executive with 35+ years spanning MICE, experiential marketing, exhibitions, and the built environment — across Singapore, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, and Europe.

Now back home in Singapore after two decades in the Middle East, I'm seeking my next leadership role — in MICE, events, business development, or executive management — where world-class experience meets new ambition.
